Commercial Tenant Improvements in Rocklin

Office, retail, restaurant, and light commercial renovations managed around inspections, deadlines, and business needs.

Who this service is for

Business owners, landlords, and property managers who need a contractor that can coordinate trades, communicate clearly, and respect opening dates.

Project process

  • Site walk and scope review.
  • Budget, schedule, and landlord coordination.
  • Proposal, permit path, and trade planning.
  • Demo, framing, rough trades, finishes, inspections, and punch list.

Timeline expectations

Commercial tenant improvements commonly take 4 to 12 weeks depending on plan review, inspections, material lead times, and business constraints.

Project examples

Common scopes include office buildouts, retail refreshes, restroom upgrades, lighting improvements, partitions, flooring, paint, and millwork.
