Kitchen remodeling for homeowners who want a durable, high-end result.

Designed around daily life and long-term value

A premium kitchen remodel has to solve more than finishes. We help evaluate structure, storage, lighting, traffic flow, plumbing, electrical capacity, appliance clearances, and realistic construction sequencing before demolition starts.

Best for

  • Luxury kitchen upgrades and full layout changes.
  • Open-concept structural modifications.
  • Custom cabinetry, stone counters, tile, lighting, flooring, and appliance packages.
  • Older homes that need electrical, plumbing, or framing updates during the remodel.

Process

Consultation, measurements, allowance planning, design coordination, permit review, demolition, rough trades, inspections, cabinetry, surfaces, finish work, punch list, and final walkthrough.

Kitchen FAQ

Most full kitchen remodels take eight to fourteen weeks of construction after selections and materials are ready. Permit timelines vary by jurisdiction and structural scope.
