Kitchen Remodeling in Rocklin

Custom kitchen remodels with thoughtful layouts, durable finishes, and clear communication from estimate to walkthrough.

Who this service is for

Homeowners who want a better kitchen layout, upgraded cabinetry, improved lighting, more storage, and a finished space that supports daily life and resale value.

Project process

  • Consultation and site review.
  • Measurements, material direction, and budget alignment.
  • Written proposal and schedule planning.
  • Demo, rough trades, cabinetry, surfaces, tile, and finish work.
  • Final walkthrough and warranty closeout.

Timeline expectations

Most kitchen remodels take 5 to 10 weeks once materials are ready. Structural changes, custom cabinetry, and permit requirements can extend the schedule.

Project examples

Recent kitchen projects include island expansions, cabinet replacement, lighting upgrades, wall openings, quartz counters, tile backsplashes, and appliance coordination.

FAQs

Can the kitchen layout change? Yes. We review framing, plumbing, electrical, appliance clearances, and permit requirements before confirming scope.

Do you help with selections? Yes. We can guide cabinetry, surfaces, tile, lighting, flooring, and fixture decisions.
