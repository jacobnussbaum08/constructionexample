Home Additions in Rocklin

Expand your home with a contractor who understands structure, permits, exterior tie-ins, and finish continuity.

Who this service is for

Homeowners who need more space for family, work, guests, aging parents, or daily comfort without leaving the neighborhood they like.

Project process

  • Feasibility conversation and site review.
  • Design coordination, budget range, and schedule planning.
  • Permit preparation and trade coordination.
  • Foundation, framing, roofing, rough trades, exterior finish, and interiors.
  • Final walkthrough and closeout.

Timeline expectations

Home additions commonly take 8 to 20 weeks depending on design, permitting, foundation, structural work, and finish level.

Project examples

Recent additions include primary suites, family room expansions, garage additions, office spaces, and connected indoor-outdoor living areas.

FAQs

Will the addition match the existing home? We plan rooflines, siding, windows, trim, flooring transitions, and interior finishes so the new space feels intentional.

Can we stay in the home? In many cases, yes. We discuss phasing, protection, access, utilities, and dust control before work begins.
