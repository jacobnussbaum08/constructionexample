Bathroom Remodeling in Rocklin

Primary suites, hall baths, powder rooms, and walk-in showers built with strong waterproofing and polished finish details.

Who this service is for

Homeowners who want a cleaner, brighter, more durable bathroom with better storage, ventilation, fixtures, tile, and long-term protection behind the walls.

Project process

  • Scope review and measurements.
  • Fixture, tile, vanity, and lighting planning.
  • Written proposal and schedule.
  • Demo, plumbing, electrical, waterproofing, tile, glass, paint, and trim.
  • Final walkthrough.

Timeline expectations

Bathroom remodels typically take 3 to 7 weeks depending on tile complexity, glass lead times, plumbing changes, and inspection needs.

Project examples

Common upgrades include curbless showers, freestanding tubs, double vanities, large-format tile, better lighting, exhaust fans, and custom niches.

FAQs

Do you handle waterproofing? Yes. Shower and wet-area assemblies are planned carefully before tile installation.

Can you improve storage? Yes. We can add vanities, linen storage, niches, shelving, and better fixture layouts.
