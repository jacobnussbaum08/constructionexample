ADU Construction in Rocklin

Detached ADUs, garage conversions, guest spaces, and rental-ready units planned around property access, privacy, utilities, and code requirements.

Who this service is for

Property owners who want flexible family housing, guest space, aging-parent options, rental income, or a more useful backyard structure.

Project process

  • Site review, access planning, and feasibility conversation.
  • Budget range, plan coordination, and utility review.
  • Permitting and schedule setup.
  • Foundation or conversion work, framing, rough trades, finishes, and inspections.
  • Turnover and final walkthrough.

Timeline expectations

ADU projects often take 12 to 24 weeks depending on plan readiness, permit review, utility work, site access, and finish level.

Project examples

Recent ADU scopes include detached cottages, compact kitchens, full bathrooms, garage conversions, private entries, and low-maintenance finishes.

FAQs

Can my lot fit an ADU? We review access, setbacks, utilities, drainage, parking, and privacy before recommending next steps.

Can an ADU be rented? Rental rules vary by city and property type. We help clarify practical questions before construction planning moves forward.
