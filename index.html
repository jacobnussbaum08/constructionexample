Questions owners ask first

Practical answers before you invite anyone into your project.

How soon can you start?

Start dates depend on scope, permits, material lead times, and current production capacity. After the consultation, we give you a realistic preconstruction and construction timeline.

Will you work with my architect or designer?

Yes. We regularly collaborate with outside design professionals and can also connect you with trusted partners when the project needs drawings or finish guidance.

How do change orders work?

Changes are documented in writing with cost and schedule impact before the work moves forward, so there are no vague verbal approvals.

Do you warranty your work?

Yes. Covered workmanship items are backed by a written warranty, and manufacturer warranties apply to qualifying materials and systems.
